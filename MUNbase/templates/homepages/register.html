Why MUNster?

For Delegates: Tired of Filling repititive forms for Delegate Registrations, browsing social media to look for good MUNs, and being added to spammy EB application groups? MUNbase solves all these problems for delegates by allowing them to build an online profile, whih you can use to store all your experience, browse for MUNs, register for various MUNs and even keep up to date with all their announcements, through one common platform, eliminating all the hassle

For MUN Organizers: MUNster offers a common transparent platform for MUN Organizers and Delegates to interact. No longer do you have to rely on Whatsapp Forwards, or Database Collaborations to get more people to register for your MUN! Moreover, MUNster lets you manage all your registrations from one common point, and even lets you make announcements, so delegate users are aware about your MUN's activities
